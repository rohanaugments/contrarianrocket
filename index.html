The process

Five stages.
Each one ships something real.

A rocket needs a payload worth launching, fuel to break orbit, a trajectory no one else is taking, and stages that drop off once their work is done. Brand-building maps the same way.

01

Payload

What's worth launching
60 min · live 1:1

A guided biographical conversation, looking for the common thread.

We walk through the milestones of your life — the moments that mattered, the turns you took, the things you built, the things you walked away from. While you talk, I listen for the thread that connects them. The thing that's been showing up across every chapter, whether you've noticed it or not.

That thread is the intersection of five things — your bliss, what comes naturally to you, what you'd do unpaid, what you've mastered, and what you want to be doing in the future.

You leave with: One clean sentence — the single intersection. Your Payload. Everything that follows is built on top of it.
02

Fuel

The number that funds it
45 min · live 1:1

A sharp, direct conversation about money.

The question every other brand coach skips: how much do you actually want to earn? Not a vague "financial freedom" answer. A real number you'd commit to. And then the floor — the monthly minimum below which the brand is failing.

Two numbers. The ambition and the floor. Together they form the economic spine of the brand — the constraint that filters every later decision.

You leave with: Your annual ambition and your monthly minimum. Specific. Honest. Not flinched.
03

Trajectory

Positioning, business model, offer
90 min · live 1:1 · I present

I bring you the answer, not a menu of options.

Between Stage 2 and Stage 3 I take your Payload and your Fuel and build the business around it. I arrive at the session with one recommended trajectory — not three.

  • A one-line positioning statement
  • The contrarian point of view that drives it — what your industry says vs. what you'll say
  • A business model and core offer, sized to hit your Fuel number
  • A projected revenue path from monthly minimum to annual ambition

You react. We sharpen together in the room.

You leave with: Clarity on what you're selling, to whom, against whom, and at what price. Strategy turned into a sellable business.
04

Ignition

The contrarian content engine
90 min · live 1:1

We turn your brand into something that gets remembered — by saying what others won't.

The brand promised on the homepage is built here. Together we surface the contrarian beliefs you hold against your industry — the things common wisdom in your space gets wrong, that you've been too polite to say. Those beliefs become the fuel for your content.

  • Surface the contrarian beliefs that anchor your point of view
  • Design the content system — pillars, formats, cadence, where it lives
  • Design the content strategy — how the beliefs sequence over weeks
  • Brief you on the homework: build out the populated content plan
You leave with: A list of contrarian beliefs, a content system, and a content strategy. Your homework: turn it into a full content plan you'll launch in Stage 5.
05

Orbit

After you've launched
90 min · live 1:1 · after launch

You publish on your own. Then we sit down and make you sustainable.

This is the contrarian way to end a brand sprint. Other coaches "launch with you," which creates dependence. I want you to publish your website and your first wave of content without me — so you've already proven you can. Then we sit down to make you better at doing it alone.

  • Debrief the launch — what landed, what didn't, what surprised you
  • Teach the fastest way to make contrarian content, so the rhythm holds
  • Show you how the content engine actually converts to income against your Fuel number
By the end of Stage 5: Website live. Offer live. Content live. And the skill to keep it all in orbit without me.

Why this works.
